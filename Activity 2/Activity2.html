Recognize someone, Then play a custom sound!

Connect to Robot:

Train Faces:

Before Misty can recognize individuals, she must be able to get a good look at them.

Train a new face by adding a name and clicking 'Train'.

The name can only be alphanumeric characters and may not contain spaces.

Look for People:

Have Misty look for people she knows, then play a specific audio file when a they are recognized.
